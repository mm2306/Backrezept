Gingerbread Recipe

Ingredients Steps Extra steps




Ingredients

Flour:   600g
Brown sugar:   530g
Gingerbread spice:   1 Packung
Kakao powder:   2 EL
Sunflower oil:   0.5 dl
Baking powder:   2 EL
Milk:   6 dl





Steps


Step 1
Mix all ingredients in a bowl
Step 2
After mixing, bake on a greased baking tray for 30 minutes in an oven preheated to 200° C on top and bottom heat
Step 3
Remove from the oven and cut into pieces.


DONE! 





extra steps


Extra chopped chocolate
You can also add pieces of chocolate to the mixture
Chocolate icing
At the end, before cutting into pieces, you can add chocolate icing to the gingerbread.
